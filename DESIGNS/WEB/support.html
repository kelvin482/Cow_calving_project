Support FAQ

Questions Around Help, Escalation, And Follow-Up

Use these answers to decide when this page should connect you to the guide, the checklist, or outside help.

When should support replace self-management? ▼
Support should take priority when progress stops, the presentation appears abnormal, or the calf or cow shows signs that the situation is no longer routine.
What should I record before calling for help? ▼
Record timing, observed signs, any assistance already given, the cow identity, and the calf condition. That improves the quality of the response.
How does this page fit with the guide? ▼
The guide explains what is happening. This page helps you decide how to respond and who should be involved next.
Why split support into its own page? ▼
It reduces overload on the main pages and makes the project feel like a structured site instead of one repeated long document.
