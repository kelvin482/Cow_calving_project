Built For Dairy Farmers

One clearer path from heat signs to safe calving follow-up

CowCalving.farm is built to help farmers record the dates that matter, understand what happens next, and keep every cow moving through breeding, pregnancy, and calving with less guesswork.

The home page should explain that value fast: record what you observe, see the next important milestone, and know when to prepare on your own or bring in veterinary support.

Our Goal

Give every farmer one simple record and one clear next step

Instead of scattered notes and missed follow-up dates, the platform brings the reproductive story of each cow into one place. Farmers can start from heat, record insemination, watch for pregnancy progress, and prepare for calving with a timeline they can understand quickly.

Record the key event once. The calendar and next actions update around the date that matters most.
See the next milestone clearly. Upcoming checks, close-up watch, and due dates stay visible without crowding the page.
Bring in support at the right time. When a routine case changes, the farmer already knows where to go next for help.
