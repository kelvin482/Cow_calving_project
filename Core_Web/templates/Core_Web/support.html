Support FAQ

When should support replace self-management?

When progress stalls, the calf is weak, the cow is exhausted, presentation is abnormal, or the farm is no longer confident the case is routine.

Support FAQ

How does this page fit with the guide?

The guide explains what is happening. Support helps the farmer decide how to respond, who to contact, and what the next follow-up should be.
